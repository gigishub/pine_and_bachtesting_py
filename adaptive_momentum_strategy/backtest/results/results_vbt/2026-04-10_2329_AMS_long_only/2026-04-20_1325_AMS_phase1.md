## §5. Threshold Sweep

_How to read:_
```
'Robust (≥20%)' line  : coin ✅ if ≥ 20% of its combos pass — robustness floor
'Any combo passes' line       : coin ✅ if ≥ 1 combo passes — matches Top Combos table

Gap between lines = coins with passing combos below the robustness floor
  (they appear in Top Combos but NOT counted as robust in the symbol pass rate)

steep drop  →  threshold sensitive  →  small change excludes many coins
flat line   →  stable zone  →  safe to move threshold here
```
